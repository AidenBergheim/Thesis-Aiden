Writing expressions for $\boldsymbol{\bar{\psi}}(t)$ and $\boldsymbol{\psi}(t)$ in terms of $\beta(t)$

$$\boldsymbol{\bar{\psi}}(t) = \begin{bmatrix} \cos(\beta(t)) \\ \sin(\beta(t)) \end{bmatrix}$$
$$\boldsymbol{\psi}(t) = \begin{bmatrix} \cos\left(\beta(t) - \frac{3\pi}{2}\right) \\ \sin\left(\beta(t) - \frac{3\pi}{2}\right) \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -\sin(\beta(t)) \\ \cos(\beta(t)) \end{bmatrix}.$$

Simplifications

$\psi^\top(t)\bar{\psi}(t)=-\sin(\beta(t))\cos(\beta(t))+\sin(\beta(t))\cos(\beta(t))=0$

$\psi^\top(t)\psi(t)=\cos^2(\beta(t))+\sin^2(\beta(t))=1$

$\boldsymbol{\psi}^\perp(t)=-\bar{\boldsymbol{\psi}}(t)$
