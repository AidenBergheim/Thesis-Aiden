The targets centroid is stationary, that is, $\boldsymbol{\dot{c}}(t)=0$ for all $t \geq 0$.
# Proof
---
Using **Background 4**, we have that
$$\boldsymbol{\dot{c}}(t)=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \boldsymbol{\dot{x}}_i(t).$$
From **Assumption 2** it follows that 

$$\boldsymbol{\dot{c}}(t)=\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \boldsymbol{\dot{x}}_i(t)=0.$$
